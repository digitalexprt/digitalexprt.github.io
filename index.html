Operating model

Transformation without theater.

DigitalExprt uses a pragmatic sequence: make the drag visible, prove a better motion in a real team, then institutionalize the discipline so gains survive contact with reality.

01

Diagnose the drag

Map the pressure points across architecture, team behavior, product flow, and AI usage so leadership can see the real shape of the problem.

Outcome: boardroom-visible risk and direction.

02

Prove the motion

Apply TDN, test-first thinking, and focused coaching inside a pilot team to create a credible, repeatable success pattern.

Outcome: a lighthouse result with lower blast radius.

03

Institutionalize the discipline

Embed governance, leadership habits, and engineering standards so AI adoption improves the system rather than accelerating its decay.

Outcome: durable velocity instead of temporary heroics.

Founder

DigitalExprt is led by Alex Bunardzic.

Alex Bunardzic is a business velocity architect and software systems leader with 34+ years of designing and delivering software. His work spans Raymond James, WorkSafeBC, and enterprise platforms in fintech, healthcare, and other demanding delivery environments.

He created Test-Driven Navigation (TDN) to help organizations use AI as a disciplined engineering partner instead of a faster route to hidden risk. He also organizes practical leadership and workshop engagements focused on refactoring safely, clarifying direction before execution, and turning engineering quality into a business advantage.
